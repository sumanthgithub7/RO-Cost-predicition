Project Overview

This project leverages advanced machine learning techniques to predict capital costs of RO desalination plants, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven decisions for sustainable water solutions. By combining comprehensive data analysis with cutting-edge AI technology, we're revolutionizing how water management projects are planned and executed.

Our Methodology

Technical Approach

Data Collection & Preprocessing

Gathered comprehensive dataset of 1806 RO plants worldwide, including plant specifications, costs, and operational parameters.

Model Development

Implemented a deep neural network with multiple dense layers, dropout for overfitting prevention, and ReLU activation.

Model Training

Utilized early stopping, learning rate scheduling, and cross-validation for optimal performance.

Validation & Testing

Rigorous validation with train-test split and comprehensive error analysis using MAE and RMSE metrics.
